Sara's Favorite Movies

The Best Movie's I've ever Seen!

Fievel: An American Tail

I loved this movie growing up. My favorite song growing up was "Somewhere Out There."

Mad Love

A story of a woman suffering from manic depression (1990's Drew Barrymore) who captures the attention of her lakeside neighbor (Chris Odonnel). She convinces him to sweep her away to Mexico. It's a dramatic 90s teen romance movie. Worth a watch. I haven't seen it in forever, so maybe I should rewatch to ensure I still like it.

Big Fish

Ewen McGreggor has my heart forever, and the visuals of this film directed by Tim Burton are captivating
